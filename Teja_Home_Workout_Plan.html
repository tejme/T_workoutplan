Teja's Home Workout Plan

This is your personalized weekly home workout plan. Follow each day's routine and track your progress!

Day 1 - Full Body Strength

  • Push-ups – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Plank – 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Glute Bridges – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers – 3 sets x 20 reps

Day 2 - Cardio & Core

  • Jumping Jacks – 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • High Knees – 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Crunches – 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Plank to Push-up – 3 sets x 10 reps

Day 3 - Lower Body

  • Lunges – 3 sets x 12 reps each leg
  • Wall Sit – 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Step-ups – 3 sets x 10 reps each leg
  • Calf Raises – 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Squat Pulses – 3 sets x 15 reps

Day 4 - Active Recovery

  • Light Yoga or Stretching – 20 mins
  • Walking – 30 mins

Day 5 - Upper Body + Core

  • Incline Push-ups – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Superman Hold – 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Russian Twists – 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Side Plank – 2 sets x 30 seconds each side

Day 6 - HIIT Workout

  • 20s Work / 10s Rest (Repeat 3x):
  • Jump Squats
  • Burpees
  • High Knees
  • Push-ups
  • Mountain Climbers

Day 7 - Rest

  • Full rest or light stretching
